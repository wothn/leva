income
noun [ C or U ]
uk
/ˈɪŋ.kʌm/ us
/ˈɪn.kʌm/
B2
money that is earned from doing work or received from investments
收入，收益
Average incomes have risen by 4.5 percent over the past year. 人均收入比去年上涨了4.5%。
More help is needed for people on low incomes. 低收入人群需要更多的帮助。
I haven't had much income from my stocks and shares this year. 今年我在股票上没有多少收益。
a company's profit in a particular period of time
（公司营业上的）收益
The company's income has greatly improved: profit rose more than 50 percent last year. 公司收入大为改善，去年的利润上涨了50%。
